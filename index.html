✓ Councillor CAN be held responsible for:
🏗 Roads, potholes & street lights
💧 Water & sewage services
🗑 Rubbish collection
🏠 RDP housing waiting lists
🛒 Local business permits & tenders
🏞 Parks, libraries & community halls
👮 Local law enforcement & neighbourhood watches
✗ Councillor CANNOT control:
🏫 Schools & education (WCED — provincial)
🚕 Taxi licensing & routes (provincial/national)
⚡ Load shedding (Eskom — national)
🏥 Hospitals & clinics (provincial)
🚓 SAPS police (national)
💼 Social grants (SASSA — national)
💡 Score parties only on what local government can actually do. Don't punish a councillor for Eskom or taxi problems — take those up with your provincial or national representatives.
